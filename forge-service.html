GOOD SOURCE FOOTAGE

The beta is deliberately selective.

It works best with one clear subject, a stable or mostly stable camera, enough surrounding space, and no rapid cuts. I review the source before confirming the job instead of promising automatic tracking for arbitrary footage.

Good fit

  • One coach or athlete
  • 6–60 seconds of selected footage
  • Stable camera
  • Clear usage permission
  • Approved title and caption text

Separate quote

  • Long raw footage requiring highlight selection
  • Multi-person tracking
  • Rapid cuts and large subject movement
  • Complex motion graphics
  • Publishing and content strategy
