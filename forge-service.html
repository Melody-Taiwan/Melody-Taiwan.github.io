BEST-FIT WORKFLOW

Built for approved masters, not open-ended creative editing.

The pilot is designed for teams that already have an approved edit and need repeatable platform, language, or brand versions. Every source batch is reviewed before the scope is confirmed.

Good fit

  • 1–3 approved master videos
  • Clear brand assets and usage rights
  • Approved title, subtitle, and translation copy
  • Stable layouts suitable for repeatable versioning
  • A real batch that currently requires manual duplication

Not included in the pilot

  • Hook writing or creative concept development
  • Trend research, music selection, or sound design
  • Major pacing changes or narrative re-editing
  • Professional color grading or visual cleanup
  • Translation authorship, voice cloning, or cultural adaptation
