WHY DEVELOPMENT PAUSED

A working pipeline is not automatically a valuable business.

Market review showed that single-clip resizing is price-sensitive, while higher-value creative operations require broader production, campaign, localization, governance, and performance capabilities.

What v1 proves

  • End-to-end Python and FFmpeg delivery
  • Configuration-driven rendering
  • Consistent branding and subtitle placement
  • Automated media QA
  • Documented, reproducible engineering work

What v1 does not claim

  • Creative hook or campaign strategy
  • Advanced pacing, sound design, or color grading
  • Production-scale localization operations
  • Advertising performance optimization
  • Universal support for arbitrary footage

PROJECT STATUS

Paused, documented, and ready to reactivate only when the economics justify it.
